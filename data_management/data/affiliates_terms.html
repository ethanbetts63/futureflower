2. Commission Structure

2.1 Non-Delivery Partners:

These terms apply specifically to non-delivery partners (Affiliates).

2.2 The "Rule of Three":

Commission is paid only on the first three (3) successful deliveries per unique referred customer. After the third delivery, no further commission is earned on that customer.

2.3 Tiered Earnings:

Commission amounts are determined by the value of the bouquet budget selected by the customer.

2.4 Commission Determination:

The commission rate and structure applied to an order are those in effect at the time the order is delivered, not at the time the referral was originally made. FutureFlower reserves the right to modify these tiers and rates for all future orders at its discretion.

3.1 Eligible Orders:

3.2 Exclusions:

4.2 Approval Window:

Commissions are marked as "Pending" until delivery is confirmed and the refund window has passed.

4.3 Payout Schedule:

Payments are generally processed within 14 days of a confirmed delivery, provided the minimum payout threshold of $10 has been met.

4.4 Offsets:

If a commission has been paid on an order that is subsequently charged back or refunded, FutureFlower reserves the right to deduct that amount from future earnings.
